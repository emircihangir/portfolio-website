Rebar Info
Release Date: May 2024
An application developed to easily perform weight and cross-section calculations and diameter conversion calculations in a practical way for diameters from Ø8 to Ø50 for rebar. Thanks to this application, Civil Engineers will be able to easily make these calculations, which they always need, with their smartphones. Depending on the feedback from users, different calculations that may be needed regarding rebar will be added and developed to the application.
