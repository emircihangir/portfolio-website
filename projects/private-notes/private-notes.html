Private Notes
Currently in testing.
Private Notes is a simple, privacy-first app for writing and storing your personal notes. It works entirely offline, with no cloud sync or internet access, so your data never leaves your device. Everything is protected using AES-256 encryption — the same standard trusted by security professionals — ensuring your notes stay private and secure. The app is open source, with no accounts, no ads, and no tracking, giving you a clean, minimal space where you stay in full control of your information.
